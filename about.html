Nuestra Historia

Revol was born in 2017 as the result of the passion of a group of friends for fashion. From our beginnings we wanted mix the elegance of the past with contemporary elements to make fashionable footwear without losing the essence of the product. Founded with passion and love for details, our brand was born from a fascination with the timeless charm of vintage fashion.

Some of the elements that characterize our products are unique designs, craftsmanship and durability and quality of the product. Our team of designers and artisans work non-stop to fusing design with the comfort and versatility you expect from modern shoes. Each pair is a work master that incorporates traditional techniques with a touch of contemporary innovation.
Always situated between the formal and the informal, we seek confusion and harmony in the union of diverse antagonistic worlds. Building from the feet up, always taking our inspiration history and the events of the past that have influenced us so much today.
After years developing products and researching the market, we are aware that our brand is not for everyone but we don't pretend to be either. Our shoes are aimed at people who want to be unique and stand out from the rest due to its design. That is why numerous well-known brands such as Converse or Nike have decided a long time ago to collaborate with us and give a new approach to their designs.

Bocetos

Los bocetos son el punto de partida, dónde la imaginación se convierte en líneas y formas. Cada trazo cuenta una historia y en cada boceto se encuentra la promesa de un zapato único y especial.

TALLER

Los bocetos cobran vida. La visión se materializa en cada costura, corte y detalle. Cada línea se transforma en un prototipo tangible.

RESULTADO FINAL

Cada par es una obra de arte en si misma, donde la creatividad y la artesanía se fusionan en un producto especial, donde la belleza, la calidad y el estilo se unen en un zapato con mucha durabilidad.
